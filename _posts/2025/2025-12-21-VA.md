End of term means frosty rides to work, xmas lunches and this year a visit to the V&A storehouse. The week concluded with an interestsing PhD viva on Robotic Cities and a book on Anglepoise Lamps.

<!--more-->

<a data-flickr-embed="true" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pseudonomad/54998010378/in/datetaken/" title="V&amp;A storehouse"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54998010378_1660644e33_c.jpg" width="100%" alt="V&amp;A storehouse"/></a>
