Starting to juggle projects now as a few start to overlap. Had some success with internal innovation funding meaning a side project has come to life. We are working with Nuna Studio on a really cool rainwater capture project for slowing down roof rainwater running into sewers. More on this in a future week but it involves solar panels, batteries and lifting water up 7m with a small pump.
