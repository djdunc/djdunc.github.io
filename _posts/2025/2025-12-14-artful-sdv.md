Hornsey Townhall [reopened this week](https://haringey.gov.uk/news/20251204/community-access-safeguarded-hornsey-town-hall). Lots of civic clocks, wood paneling and art deco fixtures and fittings. Also joined "[How to Live An Artful Life](https://www.penguin.co.uk/books/471552/how-to-live-an-artful-life-by-hessel-katy/9781529155204)" with Katy Hessel in the council chambers. The week ended with SDV crits and panettone.
