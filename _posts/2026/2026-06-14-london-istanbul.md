Some fun creating an interactive, web dashboard that maps and visualizes [Rohan's](https://www.strava.com/athletes/108315829) 44-day cycle journey from London to Istanbul (April 24th – June 6th, 2026). I had been following on a daily basis to enviously see where he had been. As he neared the completion of the journey I found myself wanting to see the scale of where he had ridden. I thought the process of accessing Strava data would be easier than it turned out to be.

<!--more-->

5 hours later (I had budgeted 1!) I had managed to download GPS traces, fill in some missing gaps with estimated routes (via Strava) and plotted onto a map. Pretty happy with how this shows the scale of the journey and some of the overall stats.

The repo with how this was made is on [GitHub](https://github.com/djdunc/london-istanbul). A [live demo is on this site](https://www.iot.io/projects/london-istanbul/)
