Similar to [last year](https://www.iot.io/blog/2024/12/28/xmas.html) 2025 in numbers is way of looking back at what I have done and what mattered to me. I set a few goals at the start of the start of 2025 (cycling and swimming) and other items I follow since I find it interesting to look back to see how the patterns change over the years. My main goal this year was to exercise every day. That ranged from a 4 hour 70k cycle to a 20 minute walk around the block. The key was to do something daily. So here are the stats.

<!--more-->

In addition to the "do something every day", the two main exercise goals were to cycle 100km a week and swim more than 120,000m over the year (which I managed [in September](https://www.iot.io/blog/2025/09/07/120000.html)).
