# UCL200 Green Wall and Canopy Project

Below is a list of the planned sensors being installed. The overview sketch shows the approximate suggested locations, the table of data and images are provided as a guide for what will be installed.

Overview of locations:

![sketch](/gordonstreetsensors.jpeg)

## Weather Station

A Davis VP2 has been proposed mounted on a tripod or pole attached to wall on top of green canopy. [Product link](https://www.davisinstruments.com/pages/vantage-pro2).

![Davis VP2](/davisweather.jpeg)

## VS121 - People Counter

A Milesight VS121 people counter to be installed under canopy. [Product Link](https://www.milesight.com/iot/product/lorawan-sensor/vs121). (Note: square sensor in middle of image shown is VS121, against a smoke alarm for size comparison)

![VS121](/vs121.jpeg)

## Clover Soil Sensor

A Tektelic clover soil moisture sensor is proposed for monitoring soil moisture levels. [Product Link](https://tektelic.com/products/sensors/clover-agriculture-sensor/).

![Clover Soil Sensor](/soil.jpeg)

## RSTP / Acoupi

Both these devices are bespoke Raspberry Pi based devices with microphone and / or camera. Two existing installations are shown to give an indication of appearance. 

![Acoupi](/echobox.jpeg)

Echobox Acoustic Sensor on lamp post (Bat / Bird Monitor)

![Birdbox](/birdbox.jpeg)

Birdbox Visual Sensor (Camera and Microphone) - shown here as blue "Bird House"

## Urban Heat Island

A bespoke UHI sensor. [Project Page](https://mandymadongyi.github.io/UrbanHeatSense.IoT/about.html).

![Urban Heat Island Sensor](/UHI.jpeg)
